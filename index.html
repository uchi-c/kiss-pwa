Why Sexual Health Education Matters

Sexual health is not only about avoiding disease. It is also about informed decision-making, communication, consent, protection, and confidence.

Young adults benefit when information is accurate, visual, and easy to access without stigma.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions?

Clear answers about condoms, correct use, and support access.

Are condoms 100% effective?+
No. Correct and consistent use greatly improves protection, but no method is perfect.
Why should I check the expiry date?+
Expired condoms may weaken over time, reducing reliability and safety.
Why add images to the condom use guide?+
Visual instruction makes the process clearer, faster to understand, and easier to remember.
What does the PWA upgrade do?+
It allows the site to be installed like an app on supported devices, improving presentation quality and usability.
